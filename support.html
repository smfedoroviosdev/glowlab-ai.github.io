If lullabies are silent

Bring the sound back before your baby wakes.

  • Make sure the device isn't muted and system volume is above 50%.
  • Open GlowLab AI, pause the lullaby, wait three seconds, then press play.
  • Restart your device if audio is missing in other apps too.

Restore Premium lullabies

Use App Store purchase history to unlock existing purchases.

  • Open GlowLab AI -> Settings -> Premium.
  • Tap "Restore purchases" and confirm with your Apple ID.
  • Ensure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
