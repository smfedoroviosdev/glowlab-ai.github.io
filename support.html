If the audio is silent

Follow these steps to restore playback.

  • Make sure the device is not muted and system volume is above 50%.
  • Open GlowLab AI, pause the session, and start it again after 3 seconds.
  • Restart your device if the issue appears across other apps.

Restore Premium access

Use App Store purchase history to unlock existing purchases.

  • Open GlowLab AI -> Settings -> Premium.
  • Tap "Restore purchases" and confirm with your Apple ID.
  • Ensure you are signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
