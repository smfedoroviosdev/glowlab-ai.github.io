GlowLab AI Privacy Policy

We keep your family's bedtime moments private and on your device.

1. What this policy covers

This privacy policy explains how GlowLab AI Baby Sleep handles information within the iOS and watchOS applications and any companion websites.

2. Data GlowLab AI does not collect

GlowLab AI does not require you to create an account and does not collect personal data about you or your baby. Lullaby choices, timers, and nursery preferences remain on your device.

3. On-device processing

Custom mixes, playback states, and night-light visuals are stored in Apple-protected storage on your device. If you delete the app, iOS removes these items automatically. GlowLab AI does not transmit audio or baby information to external servers.

4. Purchases and subscriptions

If you choose to buy GlowLab AI Premium, the transaction is handled by Apple using your App Store account. We receive confirmation of the purchase status only; no billing or payment details are shared with us.

5. Diagnostics and analytics

We rely on aggregated, anonymous analytics provided by Apple, such as crash reports and basic usage trends, to understand app performance. You can disable sharing diagnostics with developers in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Analytics & Improvements.

6. Your choices

You can clear bedtime history and reset settings at any time inside the app. Removing GlowLab AI from your device deletes all related data. Contact us if you want to request additional privacy actions.
